# Day 14 – AI Job Red Flag Detector

## Objective

Learn how to use AI to evaluate job opportunities by analyzing a Job Description (JD) and company information, identifying potential risks, reviewing positive signals, and generating interview preparation questions.

---

# Task Performed

Today I used Claude to analyze a job opportunity using the Job Red Flag Detector.

The workflow included:

1. Collecting a Job Description (JD).
2. Gathering company information.
3. Using the provided Job Red Flag Detector prompt.
4. Uploading the JD and company details.
5. Generating a complete risk analysis report.
6. Reviewing the overall risk score.
7. Identifying red flags and positive signals.
8. Analyzing the detailed risk breakdown.
9. Reviewing the final hiring recommendation.
10. Studying the AI-generated interview questions.
11. Saving the report and screenshots.

---

# Tools Used

- Claude AI
- Job Description (JD)
- Company Information
- Job Red Flag Detector Prompt

---

# Analysis Performed

The AI evaluated the opportunity based on:

- Company credibility
- Job description quality
- Compensation transparency
- Technical requirements
- Career growth potential
- Work culture indicators
- Hiring process
- Overall job risk score

---

# Report Summary

The generated report included:

- Overall Risk Score
- Red Flags
- Positive Signals
- Risk Breakdown
- Final Verdict
- Suggested Interview Questions
- Recommendations before applying

---

# Key Learnings

- AI can quickly evaluate the quality of a job opportunity.
- Reviewing company credibility helps avoid risky job offers.
- A structured risk analysis improves decision-making.
- Interview questions generated by AI help prepare for technical discussions.
- Reading beyond the job title is essential before applying.

---

# My Reflection

Today's activity demonstrated how AI can assist in evaluating job opportunities beyond the job description. The generated report highlighted important factors such as company credibility, potential risks, and interview preparation, making it easier to make informed career decisions.
