# Day 13 – AI Job Discovery with Claude Connectors

## Objective

Learn how to use Claude Connectors to discover relevant job opportunities, analyze skill gaps, review market demand, and build a personalized job application strategy.

---

# Task Performed

Today I used Claude Connectors with Indeed to discover job opportunities that match my skills and career goals.

The workflow included:

1. Connected the Indeed account using Claude Connectors.
2. Created my professional profile.
3. Defined my target job search criteria.
4. Allowed Claude to search for matching jobs.
5. Reviewed opportunity match scores.
6. Analyzed skill gaps.
7. Reviewed market demand insights.
8. Saved the generated results and screenshots.
