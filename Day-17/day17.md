# Day 17 – AI Vehicle Cost Analysis Dashboard

## Objective

Learn how to use AI to generate an interactive Vehicle Cost Analysis Dashboard by combining a CSV dataset with personal vehicle information. The dashboard provides insights into fuel costs, maintenance expenses, environmental impact, and overall vehicle economics.

---

# Task Performed

Today I used Claude to build a Vehicle Cost Analysis Dashboard.

The workflow included:

1. Downloading the provided CSV dataset.
2. Gathering my vehicle information.
3. Updating the provided prompt with my vehicle details.
4. Uploading the CSV dataset into Claude.
5. Generating the complete HTML dashboard.
6. Reviewing KPI cards and cost analysis.
7. Analyzing fuel comparisons and maintenance costs.
8. Reviewing environmental impact analysis.
9. Examining E85 fuel economics and break-even calculations.
10. Reviewing interactive charts and visualizations.
11. Saving the generated HTML dashboard.
12. Opening the dashboard in a browser for testing.
13. Capturing screenshots of important sections.
