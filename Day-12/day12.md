# Day 12 – AI Job Search & Personal Branding Toolkit

## Objective

Learn how to use AI to build a complete job search and personal branding toolkit by analyzing a resume, matching it with a target role, identifying skill gaps, and generating personalized career recommendations.

---

# Task Performed

Today I used Claude to create a Job Search & Personal Branding Toolkit.

The workflow included:

1. Uploading my latest resume.
2. Selecting my target role.
3. Providing a relevant Job Description (JD).
4. Running the Job Search & Personal Branding prompt.
5. Generating the complete toolkit.
6. Reviewing ATS recommendations and skill gap analysis.
7. Running the second improvement prompt.
8. Saving all generated outputs.
9. Capturing screenshots of important sections.

---

# Tools Used

- Claude AI
- Latest Resume
- Target Job Description (JD)
- Job Search & Personal Branding Prompt

---

# Generated Outputs

The toolkit included:

- ATS Resume Evaluation
- Skill Gap Analysis
- Resume Improvement Suggestions
- Personal Branding Recommendations
- Career Development Plan
- Job Search Strategy
- Interview Preparation Tips
- Action Plan for Target Role

---

# Key Learnings

- AI can analyze resumes against specific job descriptions.
- Personal branding plays an important role in job searching.
- Skill gap analysis helps prioritize learning goals.
- A structured job search strategy improves preparation and confidence.
- AI can generate actionable recommendations for career growth.

---

# My Reflection

Today's activity demonstrated how AI can act as a career mentor by evaluating resumes, identifying improvement areas, and suggesting practical strategies for job searching and personal branding. The generated toolkit provides a roadmap for improving my profile and preparing for future opportunities.
