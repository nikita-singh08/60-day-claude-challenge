# Day 4 – Chain-of-Thought Prompting

## Objective

Learn how Chain-of-Thought (CoT) Prompting helps AI generate structured, personalized, and logical solutions by reasoning through a problem step by step.

---

# What is Chain-of-Thought Prompting?

Chain-of-Thought Prompting is a prompt engineering technique where the AI is instructed to reason through a problem before generating the final answer.

Instead of directly producing an output, the AI:

- Understands the user's situation.
- Breaks the problem into smaller steps.
- Analyzes strengths and weaknesses.
- Identifies skill gaps.
- Creates a structured solution.

This results in more personalized, accurate, and actionable responses.

---

# Prompt Used

I used a Chain-of-Thought prompt where Claude acted as an **Elite AI Career Strategist**.

The prompt first asked me four questions:

1. What is your current situation?
2. What skills do you currently have?
3. What is your target goal?
4. What is your target timeline?

After collecting my answers, Claude analyzed my profile and generated a personalized one-page career roadmap.

# Key Learnings

- Chain-of-Thought prompting improves reasoning quality.
- Asking structured questions before solving a problem produces better results.
- Personalized prompts generate more relevant recommendations.
- Breaking a problem into logical steps leads to actionable roadmaps.
